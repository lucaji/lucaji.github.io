StartRecApp

A multicamera remote controlling iOS App. Synced recording and time lapse support. Manual camera controls and touch parameters. Background file transfer, audio and video chat.

What is Start Rec for?

Gather many iPhones and iPads running START REC in a local wi-fi network, decide one ADMIN role and set all the remaining to be POSERs before checking-in and enter into a camera session. Now each time the session admin takes a photograph, it will be taken by all connected posers.

Use the VOCAL CHAT to communicate with people in session.

TOUCH MANUAL CAMERA CONTROLS

Activate the Touchy feature and when using the Camera, you can change focus and exposure by sliding your finger across the display.

SYNCHRONIZATION AND TIMELAPSE

SYNCHRONISITY allows you to time-bind the devices. Shooting a photo at the very exact moment on every devices (always BEWARE about where the cameras are pointing at!). Synchronisity adds functionalities that could expose your privacy if misused.

  1. Can take timed photographs and time lapse.
  2. Can record audio and video.
  3. Media file consolidation in background.
