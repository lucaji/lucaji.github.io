ATPiano Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy, together with the EULA, set forth the general rules and policies governing your use of the mobile software application “ATPiano”, (hereinafter, "the App"). Depending on your activities when using the App, you may be required to agree to additional terms and conditions.

ATPiano implements a sine-wave generator, which is touch-driven by a virtual, and scrollable, piano-like keyboard displayed on the screen.
The note frequency of the middle “A” can be manually adjusted to reflect the following four Concert Pitch frequencies: the standard 440Hz, the baroque 415Hz, the uncommon 435Hz from the 1859 French “diapason normal” standard and the highest 445Hz tuning.
