CHATea PRIVACY POLICY

CHATea (the “App”) provides this Privacy Policy to help you (the “User (s)”) make an informed decision about whether to use or continue using this software product: the App itself. If you do not agree to these practices, please do not use this software and uninstall from your device(s).

As the author is based in Italy, this document conforms to the European Union’s (EU) General Data Protection Regulation (GPDR). In this Privacy Policy it is explained what is done and how, and how you can stay safe by enforcing your choices.

Description

CHATea implements a non-encrypted Chatroom between nearby iOS devices without needing an Internet connection.

The devices are automatically discovered and added to the chatroom as soon as they became available and only when the App is in foreground. When switching to background (or during a phone call, for instance) the App disconnects from the Chatroom.

Every devices that joins or leaves the chatroom will be logged onto the main window as “connecting”, “connected”, “stand-by” and “disconnected” states.

CHATea does not implement background functionalities. CHATea transcripts are not encrypted. CHATea does not store the chat transcript permanently.

Network Infrastructure

CHATea does not need nor implement any account registration: it uses Apple’s Multipeer Framework that connects nearby iOS devices using a Wifi connection you can have access to and/or the Bluetooth interfaces (when activated) on those iOS devices the App is installed onto.

The Multipeer Connectivity framework supports the discovery of services provided by nearby devices and supports communicating with those services through message-based data, streaming data, and resources (such as files). In iOS, the framework uses infrastructure Wi-Fi networks, peer-to-peer Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth personal area networks for the underlying transport.

CHATea implements only Wifi and Bluetooth discovery and does not use your mobile network at all.

Data Collection

CHATea App’s author, DOES NOT RECEIVE NOR COLLECT NOR STORE any data of any kind about your use or interaction with the App. Your user content is not indexed nor stored anyhow in permanent way, not even in the CHATea app instances a.k.a. locally on your devices except in RAM.

Since november 2018 though, CHATea implements and uses advertising currently provided by Google AdMob to keep the price of the app free. It will be using data collection technologies by Google.

Ads served by Google can be categorized as personalized or non-personalized, both requiring consent from users in the EEA. By default, ad requests to Google serve personalized ads, with ad selection based on the user’s previously collected data. Google also supports configuring ad requests to serve non-personalized ads.

For more information please refer to Google’s Privacy Policies.

User Generated Content

The App supports composing three categories of content that can be transmitted to other nearby devices: text, audio and image (collectively “transcript”).

Text: you can type text messages directly and use emojis (no authorization needed);

Audio: you can record short time voice and sound messages by holding the microphone button (authorization is needed).

Image content is diversified in the following sub-categories as more options can be accessed by tapping the button with the plus sign (+):

Take Photo: you can take a photograph with the built in camera (authorization needed); Photo from Library: you can access the Photo Library on your device to send a photograph taken and stored into that location (authorization needed); New Sketch: you can draw hand-sketches directly onto the display and send it as image (no authorization needed).

When you push the send button (the one with the right arrow) the content is sent over to (and received by) all connected nearby devices.

Hardware Access Authorization

  • Your authorization is required in order to access the hardware camera or microphone on each device the App is installed onto, before you can use them.

  • Your authorization is required to access your Photo Library before you can send photographs taken with other Apps and stored within that location.

These authorizations can be withdrawn (or restored) anytime by navigating to the iOS Settings->Privacy->CHATea panel.

Persistence of User Content

The App uses its main window to hold the transcript, that is entirely kept in the device RAM memory, so what you see by scrolling it, actually reflects the internal content within the very device you are using.

The App does not implement any persistence store mechanism such as databases.

The App makes the user capable to easily clear and loose the transcript as it can be deleted by tapping the “Clear” button or by quitting the App from within iOS (check the version of iOS you have installed to see how to quit a running App).

Once you have cleared the transcript window or quitted the App, the data cannot be recovered.

When pressing the Send button, the transcript is meant to be transmitted and received by other nearby iOS devices running CHATea App, though. There is NO direct way to delete the transcripts from all targeted devices at once: you must physically cancel the transcript or quit the App from every devices you saw connecting.
